The Story

The Story Begins When...

It’s the year 500BC and all creatures on planet Earth face extinction. Thousands of animal’s board Noah’s Ark seeking refuge from their imminent doom. Months later, as the flood begins to subside, the Ark crashes into a remote island. EXPLOSION!

The animals wander out of the Ark to find themselves on a tropical island covered in ancient artifacts. These ancient artifacts increase the intelligence of these animals, and a new, advanced species of animals are born. Within the next 100 years, these animals create an advanced civilisation, hosting sophisticated architecture and revolutionary weaponry.

Now the animals seek to elect a new king, but each faction wishes for the king to be one of their own. Countless disputes later, chaos breaks out. Soon after a war is declared… a war for the next KING OF THE JUNGLE.
